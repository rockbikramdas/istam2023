Accommodation

A. Kalam Guest House Visitor's Block:

A number of double-bedded Guest house rooms are available (mostly sharing basis) at NIT Warangal for senior researchers on first come-first served basis.All the rooms are well furnished & equipped with the basic services required by the guests, such as Wi-Fi, cable TV, hot & cold water supply in washrooms, study table with chair, luggage rack and cupboard.

B. Hostel Accommodation:

The accommodation, mainly for the student delegates, will be arranged in well-furnished hostels DASA, 1.8K , LH . Other delegates also can opt for hostel accommodation. Hostel rooms are non-AC.

ABOUT ISTAM

The ISTAM was established in 1955 by the think tanks of the post independent India with its headquarters at IIT, Kharagpur. The society was formed with the idea of providing a common platform to the scientists, technologists and the engineers to share and discuss the current research work conducted by them individually or in groups to seek the solutions to the emerging problems for better living conditions.
